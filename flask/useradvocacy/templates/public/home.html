Welcome to the home of the User Advocacy Team!

User Advocacy Mission

The User Advocacy team is the conduit for the voice, desires, and needs of the user, ensuring that they are heard at all levels of the organization. We distill the data from various communication channels and turn these insights into actions. As a team, our goal is to ensure that we, Mozilla, never fail our users. The brand promise is our driving force. It is our mantra.

Requesting Work?

Add the appropriate member of the UA team to the bug. You can also email the UA mailing list if you are unsure who to contact.
