Google Play rating over time (all versions)

This chart shows the Google Play sentiment over time for all versions of Firefox for Android. Score is on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. The dark line is the 7-day rolling average.

Input volume over time

This chart shows the Input volume per thousand ADI over time for all versions of Firefox for Android. Color indicates the sentiment score (red = completely sad, green = completely happy). Note that Input is almost completely negative as happy users are diverted to Google Play.
