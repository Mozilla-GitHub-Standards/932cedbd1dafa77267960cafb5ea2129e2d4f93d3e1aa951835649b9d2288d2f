Google Play rating over time (all versions)

This chart shows the Google Play sentiment over time for all versions of Firefox for Android. Score is on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. The dark line is the 7-day rolling average.

Input volume over time

This chart shows the Input volume per thousand ADI over time for all versions of Firefox for Android. Note that Input is almost completely negative as happy users are diverted to Google Play so we’re not indicating sentiment with color here.

Version-specific Play rating

This chart indicates the Play rating for specific versions of Firefox for Android while they are on the release channel.

Support site visits

This chart is the number of in-product visits to the Firefox Android support site each week over the past release (in thousands). As you can see, we added a top-level link to the support site in late August which resulted in a sharp increase in users accessing the site from inside Firefox for Android.
